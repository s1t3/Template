About Catering


Tradition since 2019

They say you can’t choose who your family is but that is not the case with our company, Poke Bros. Poke Bros was founded by two friends who have become lifelong brothers through our love for creating delicious and innovative Asian cuisine. Together, we share over 35 years experience in the sushi and catering business. Poke Bros is a catering company that specializes in Asian cuisine. We have a solid reputation for delivering exceptional food and quality service at affordable prices. Only the freshest, healthiest and most delicious ingredients are used and every meal is created with our customers’ needs in mind. We offer a wide variety of catering services from corporate events to daily meal plans to intimate dinner parties to all types of social events – no event is too big or small. From the moment that you contact us, we will collaborate with you every step of the way to deliver exceptional food and service. Contact us today!
